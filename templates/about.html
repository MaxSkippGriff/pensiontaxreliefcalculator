About PensionTaxReliefCalculator.co.uk

PensionTaxReliefCalculator.co.uk is a free, independent tool for estimating UK pension tax relief for the {{ tax_year }} tax year.

The calculator helps UK taxpayers understand how much pension tax relief they are entitled to under relief at source, net pay arrangements and salary sacrifice — and what the real net cost of their pension contribution is after relief.

What it does

Enter your gross income, pension contribution amount and contribution method. The calculator estimates:

  • Basic rate relief (20%)
  • Extra higher rate relief (40% or 45%) for eligible taxpayers
  • Employee and employer NI savings for salary sacrifice
  • Effective net cost and monthly cost of your contribution

Scottish taxpayers can select their region to apply the correct Scottish income tax rates.

This tool is part of a network of free UK tax and finance calculators, including:
