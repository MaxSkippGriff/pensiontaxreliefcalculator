Privacy Policy

What data we collect

PensionTaxReliefCalculator.co.uk does not store, log or transmit any of the financial figures you enter into the calculator. All calculations happen in your browser.

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect anonymised usage statistics — page views, session duration and broad geographic location (country level). IP addresses are anonymised before processing. We do not use Google Signals or cross-site tracking.

Cookies

Google Analytics sets first-party cookies to distinguish returning visitors. No advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies are set by this site. Google AdSense may set cookies if ads are displayed.

Third-party services

  • Google Analytics — anonymised page analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — contextual advertising. Google Ad Policy
  • Google Fonts — fonts loaded from Google servers.

Your rights

As we collect no personal financial data, there is nothing to access, correct or delete. For Google Analytics opt-out, use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
